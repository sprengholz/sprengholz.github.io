Ok Google: Using virtual assistants for data collection in psychological and behavioral research
Because of the increasing popularity of voice-controlled virtual assistants, such as Amazon's Alexa and the Google Assistant, they should be considered a new medium for psychological and behavioral research. This article presents two studies analyzing the reliability and validity of data collected through the medium.
